(a ∣ (b ∣ (b ∣ b))) = (a ∣ a)
 
2.3  Lemmas L47 through L64 towards proving third Sheffer axiom
 
TheoremL47 98 Lemma for deriving the Sheffer BA axioms from the Wolfram axiom. (Contributed by NM and RV, 1-Oct-2021.)
((a ∣ a) ∣ (a ∣ b)) = a
 
TheoremL48 99 Lemma for deriving the Sheffer BA axioms from the Wolfram axiom. (Contributed by NM and RV, 1-Oct-2021.)
((a ∣ b) ∣ ((a ∣ c) ∣ b)) = b
 
TheoremL49 100 Lemma for deriving the Sheffer BA axioms from the Wolfram axiom. (Contributed by NM and RV, 1-Oct-2021.)
(a ∣ (((b ∣ c) ∣ a) ∣ c)) = ((c ∣ b) ∣ a)
