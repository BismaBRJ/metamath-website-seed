Axiom ax-A 7
Description: The Wolfram single axiom for Boolean algebra. The primary goal of this database is to prove Sheffer's 3 axioms for BA from this axiom. See theorems T1 69, T2 104, and T3 123. (Contributed by SW, 14-May-2002.)
